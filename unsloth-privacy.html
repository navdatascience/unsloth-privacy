Privacy Policy for UnSloth Chrome Extension

Last updated: March 14, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (for authentication purposes only)
  • Speech input (processed locally for transcription)
  • Generated document content

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Usage statistics of the extension features

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: Email is used solely for user authentication via Firebase
  • Transcription: Speech input is processed locally within your browser
  • Document Generation: Content is processed to create documentation
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us enhance the extension

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All speech processing is done locally in your browser
  • Authentication data is securely managed by Firebase
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties

4. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Request a copy of your data
